I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Physics at **Dartmouth College**, set to begin in 2026. I recently completed my B.S. in Physics at **Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST)**, where I was honored as an *Outstanding Graduate*.

My research lives at the intersection of **theoretical and computational plasma physics**, with a particular fascination for nonlinear phenomena inside turbulent plasmas. I am drawn to questions where mathematical elegance meets physical reality where we can actually compute, visualize, and understand.
